Year of the Tiger


“Valor without caution leads to recklessness”

If you are born in 1917, 1929, 1941, 1953, 1965, 1977, 1989,2001,2013, or 2025 you align with the Snake.

Tigers are brave, confident, competitive, unpredictable and self-confident. Tigers are likeable, but sometimes they are impulsive. Tigers will accept any challange if it has important value to them. They do not like to obey others.
